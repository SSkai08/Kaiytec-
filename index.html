About KAIYTEC Creative

We are
KAIYTEC Creative.

KAIYTEC Creative is the branding and digital marketing arm of KAIYTEC — a parent technology company whose mission is to turn ideas into real-world opportunity. We are the first active project under the KAIYTEC umbrella and the agency Sierra Leone businesses have been waiting for.

We believe every business in this country deserves the same quality of brand and marketing that multinational companies take for granted. We are here to close that gap — using human creativity, strategic thinking, and AI-powered tools.

We are not just a design shop. We are strategic creative partners — working to understand your business deeply before we touch a single design tool.

The Problem

we are fixing.

Unverified Sellers
Problem: Frequent fraud, zero accountability
→ Mandatory KYC with government ID + live photo. Admin-reviewed within 24–48 hours.
👻
No Digital Visibility
Problem: Small businesses are invisible online
→ GPS-powered profiles with search & discovery. Businesses found by nearest customers automatically.
💸
Unstructured Payments
Problem: Mobile money in private chats, no records
→ Platform-structured payments with proof upload. Admin verification. Escrow at scale.
